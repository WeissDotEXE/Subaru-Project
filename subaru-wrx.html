Subaru WRX STI 2020

Best Sport Sedan

Direct-Injection Turbocharged SUBARU BOXER® Engine

Drawn from over 40 years of intelligent performance, the unique SUBARU BOXER® engine has pistons that move side to side, canceling out each other’s vibrations so it can run smoother and more efficiently.. The 268-hp direct-injection turbocharged engine features a twin-scroll turbocharger for near-instant boost response and maximum power across a wide power band, delivering 258 pound-feet of torque from 2,000 to 5,200 RPM.

Brembo® Performance Brakes

Available high-performance Brembo 4-piston front and 2-piston rear calipers are matched to cross-drilled discs to give you confident, fade-resistant control for attacking your favorite corner.

EyeSight® Driver Assist Technology

Available EyeSight monitors traffic movement, optimizes cruise control, and warns you when you're swaying outside your lane. The Automatic Pre-Collision Braking feature can even apply full braking force and bring you to a complete stop in emergency situations
